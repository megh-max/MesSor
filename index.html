🔧 What is MesSor?

MesSor helps you complete big projects in less time. From robotics to science fair models, we provide ready-made lists of all parts needed—so you can build fast without wasting time on research.

⚡ How It Works

  • Select a Project (e.g., “Make a Solar Car”)
  • Get the full list of components and tools
  • Build it without confusion or delay

🎯 Why MesSor?

  • ✅ Save Research Time
  • ✅ No Confusion or Missed Parts
  • ✅ Easy for Beginners
  • ✅ Project-specific, Accurate & Quick
